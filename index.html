Legal Requirements and Criterias

Everyone between the ages of 18 and under the state pension age must have a valid fishing license to engage in angling in Denmark.

You can purchase a fishing licence here.

For the sake of nature, avoid using lead weights . Feeding must not be used.

If you are new to fishing in Denmark, it is strongly advised to visit the Ministry of Environment of Denmark to further check rules and regulations (fishing section in Danish only).
